The Barcelona FC will disappear

20 Aug 2023, by:Isaac Maldonado
Facebook Twitter Linkedin
The club as being called itself as the biggest in the world in the last 15 years, it is now a little more than $1,300 million shortfall.

Barcelona in the last summer doubling down style when spend more than $200 million, a team that less than a year ago was unable to meet its huge payroll, a business who produce losses of 487 million euros. Now it is selling the club’s asset after another and this barely bring a $700 million. On top of that, the project of 1.5 billion project to modernized their stadium, this through the financing of Goldman Sachs.

The 150,000 members of the club are raising their suspicions about the latest movements, they doubt the financial viability that Laporta says is being working on. Laporta was elected club president for a 2nd time after his predecessor was ousted for what amounted to the simultaneous financial and sporting collapse of the club.

Instead of paying off club debt, however, the money has largely gone toward accumulating new talent: 65 million for the Brazilian Raphina, $55 million for the French defender Jules Koundé, 50 million for Robert Lewandowski.

All this movement has been causing among the experts, fans and even people who is a little outside of the influence of the soccer have to even bet when the club finally will disappear. 2025,2026 or even more in the future. Which is your bet?
